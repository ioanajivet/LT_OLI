Invitation to participate in the research study My learning dashboard

We are constantly trying to improve your learning experience while on this platform and to better support you in achieving your goals. Through this study, we wish to evaluate the impact of a learning analytics feedback tool that offers you personalised feedback on your learning progress and learning behaviour. This study is part of a collaboration between Erasmus University of Rotterdam, TU Delft and Open University of the Netherlands.

If you wish to participate, we need your permission. Participation is voluntary and it will not affect your ability to participate in the course. The study will take place over the whole duration of the course meaning that you will have access to the feedback tool until the end of the course. Please, note you will not receive any compensation for participating in the study, but you will help us advance research in this domain. You can stop your study participation at any time without giving a reason by sending us an email (contact details below). Upon your request to terminate your participation in the study, you will not have access to the feedback tool anymore and we will remove your data from our research dataset.

Data collection and processing

For this study, we will collect data about:

  • Your interaction with the course activities - we will use this data to generate your personalised feedback that will be displayed on the feedback tool.
  • Interaction with the feedback tool - we will use this data to analyse when and how the tool was used by learners so that we can improve the tool in future iterations.
  • Survey answers - At the beginning of the course, we will ask you to answer a questionnaire that reflects your self-assessment on self-regulated learning. At the end of the course, we will ask you to evaluate the feedback tool used during the course. Filling out the surveys will require 10-20 minutes of your time.
  • Demographic data - We do not collect this demographic data but we will retrieve it from Coursera and include it in our dataset: age, gender, the highest level of education graduated and country.

Your rights, privacy and security

According to the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you can request the deletion of your data. Upon your request, we will remove all your data from the dataset used in our research. To exercise this right, please contact the research team before the end of the course mentioning your username used on the Coursera platform (contact details below). At the end of the course, all collected data will be anonymised, making it impossible to identify you within our dataset anymore. The collected data will be used only for research purposes and only the research team will have access to the data. Bounded by the GDPR, your personal data will not be disclosed or shared with other parties without additional consent. The anonymised dataset will be stored for the next ten years as required for scientific research.

My learning dashboard

How is this dashboard useful to me?

Learning with a MOOC can be challenging. This tool supports you to achieve your goals. You can (1) actively set your goal for this course and (2) select feedback indicators and monitor your progress towards your goal.

How can I change my goal and my feedback indicators?

You can adjust your goal and feedback indicators once per week. The buttons are enabled at the beginning of each week and stay enabled until you adjust your goal or feedback indicators.

Why can't I choose less than 3 or more than 6 indicators?

Limiting the number of indicators between 3 and 6, can give you plenty of information without overloading you with data.

Where do these feedback indicators come from?

The 12 feedback indicators you can choose from were selected from research literature. They all support goal achievement.

How are the feedback indicators calculated?

The values of the feedback indicators are calculated using all your activity data from the beginning of the course. The values of the indicators are updated daily.

Is my feedback real-time?

The values shown on the dashboard are updated once per day.

What data do you use to calculate the feedback indicators?

The feedback indicators are calculated using Coursera data that records your activitiy on this course pages.
